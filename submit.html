Submit Your Film

Show your work.

We're accepting submissions for all five tracks. No entry fee. No velvet rope. Just your film and a real audience in a real community.

Film submissions

We accept features, documentaries, shorts, and experimental work. Youth Track submissions must be 8–10 minutes and made by current middle or high school students. All non-English-language films must have English subtitles.

Select the track(s) that best fit your film — you may submit to more than one.

Get involved

Volunteer

We need 30–50 local volunteers for the ten-day run — greeters, venue staff, filmmaker liaisons, and tech crew. Volunteers get standby access to all screenings and an exclusive mixer with visiting filmmakers.

Get in touch →

Sponsor

Support a track, name a venue, or sponsor the shuttle that brings festival-goers from Boulder to Niwot. Local businesses can "Adopt-a-Film" — sponsor a single screening and get a shout-out before the movie and mention in the program.

Partner with us →

Founder's Club

Join the Founder's Club with a donation of $500–$1,000. You'll receive VIP badges, opening night tickets, and a commemorative print — and you'll be part of making this happen from the beginning.
