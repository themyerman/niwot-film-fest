Submit Your Film

Show your work.

We're accepting submissions for all five tracks. No entry fee. No velvet rope. Just your film and a real audience in a real community.

Submission deadline: October 1, 2026.

Film submissions

We accept features, documentaries, shorts, and experimental work. Youth Track submissions must be 15 minutes or under and made by current middle or high school students. All non-English-language films must have English subtitles.

Select the track(s) that best fit your film — you may submit to more than one.
